Introduction

I am currently a Ph.D candidate in the School of astronautic, Harbin Institute of Technology from Sep. 2015, majoring in the Aeronautical and Astronautical Science and Technology. I received my bachelor in Automation from the ShanDong University at Jun 2015. I was also an academic staff in the Autonomous System Lab (ASL), ETH from Aug. 2018 - Aug. 2019 under the funding of Chinese Scholarship Council. My research interests are mainly centered around the multi-robot system(MRS), including the cooperative and coordinated control, coverage control and planning under uncertainty and so on.
